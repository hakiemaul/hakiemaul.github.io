Mouse and Keyboard Testing App
By Aulia Hakiem

This is a simple application to determine whether your keyboard and mouse is functioning properly. This app was built on HTML using JavaScript and DOM.

Instructions:

  • To test your mouse "click" function, press the blue button.
  • To test your mouse "pointing" function, press the green button.
  • To test your keyboard, type in the text area. If you want to see your typing result, click the submit button.
