Live Your Life

By Aulia Hakiem

What to do with your spare time?

Some people think that it is important to use leisure time for activities that develop the mind, such as reading and doing crossword puzzles. Others feel that it is important to give one’s mind a rest in leisure time.

As we are human we naturally need to rest on leisure time to recover the stress of work and everyday life. In fact, every individual need to do what they want and belong to them. Personally, I prefer to be active during this time because it is feel right to me better. Moreover, people are free for what they do on their leisure time, and nobody can say what it is the best.

Some people want to relax after their day of work. These people may prefer to relax by watching movies, reading or getting a massage. People who have a physically jobs such as doctors, teachers and builders may choose these types of activities. If you are a doctor, you may feel that you want to let your body rest after work and you don’t want to do a five kilometer run after work, because you are already physically tired.

1. Take a walk.

This is a form of exercise that doesn’t take long, and you can do it anywhere — but even more important, it’s a good way to stretch your legs from sitting at your desk too long, and it gets your creative juices flowing. If you’re ever stuck for ideas, taking a walk is a good way to get unstuck.

2. Don't forget to read.

Reading is a lifelong skill, and successful people never stop reading new books. Whether it's fiction or nonfiction, books help give you a greater understanding of the world around you. They introduce you to new characters, new environments, new cultures, new philosophies, and new ideas, and might even help you build new skills (if you're reading nonfiction, at least).

3. Start learning.

Education shouldn't stop at college and restricted to a formal institution. The most succesful people take their own commitment to never stop learning. They're always incorporating of their new skills for their resumes and constantly learning from their surrounding. Informal learning can be found anywhere in this globalised world. You can always find free courses all over the web

4. Take up a hobby.

Some people put all their focus and effort into work. While work is important, one shouldn't downplay the effect of stress in our life. Finding and pursuing a hobby can help you relieve stress, put your job in perspective, and build skills that complement ones you use at work. It's a breath of fresh air that keeps you grounded.

5. Spend time with your loved ones.

We often don't realize that our job isn't everything. Focusing too much on your career is self-sabotage. If you want to be succesful in life, you must balance your work life with your personal life, and don't forget to prioritize your personal relationships. No matter how much you want to be succesful and climb the corporate ladder, you simply can't neglect your friends and families while doing so.

There you have our suggestions! Do you agree? Do you have another activity you usually do in your spare time? Let us know in the comment section below!
